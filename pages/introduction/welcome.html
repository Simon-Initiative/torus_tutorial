Welcome to OLI Torus Launchpad

The Torus Launchpad is your all-in-one hub for learning how to design, teach, and experience courses on OLI Torus platform.

How to Use Torus Launchpad

Pick Your Role

Head to the Home Page and choose your role: each role opens a tailored section of tutorials designed for your specific needs.

Explore Topics

Use the sidebar on the left to browse tutorials by role and topic.

Search Features

Type keywords in the search bar to instantly find related tutorials.

Try Picture-in-Picture

Use the Picture-in-Picture feature to keep videos floating on your screen as you build or edit your course. This lets you learn and apply steps side by side without switching tabs.

Welcome to OLI Torus Launchpad

The Torus Launchpad is your all-in-one hub for learning how to design, teach, and experience courses on OLI Torus platform.

How to Use Torus Launchpad

Pick Your Role

Head to the Get Started page and choose your role: each role opens a different section of tutorials designed for your specific needs.

Explore Topics

Use the Sidebar on the left to browse video tutorials by role and task.

Search Features

Type keywords in the Search Bar on the top bar to instantly find related video tutorials.

Try Picture-in-Picture

Use the Picture-in-Picture feature to keep videos floating on your screen as you build or edit your course. No need to switch between tabs!

Need Help or Have Feedback?

If something doesn’t work as expected, or you’d like to suggest a tutorial topic, click the Feedback button on the top bar to let us know.
